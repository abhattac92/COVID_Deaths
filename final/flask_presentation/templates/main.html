Results

We looked at and analyzed a large amount of Kickstarter data. We then used this data to try and predict the status outcome of future projects using machine learning. Here is what we found:

For our dataset we had more successful campaigns than we had thought. The median and average goals for failed projects were substantially higher. successful projects had much higher amounts pledged and more backers.



The average campaigns for failed projects were slightly longer, and their blurbs were as well. One difference is that the successful ones had longer lengths of creation to launch days.
